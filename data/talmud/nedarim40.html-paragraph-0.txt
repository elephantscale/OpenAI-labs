Nedarim 40 - Curing the sick The mitzvah of visiting the sick has no limit. How so? Does it mean that the reward for it is limitless? - Why, all mitzvot are that way! Instead, even a person of great importance should visit people of more diminutive stature. Or it could mean that there is no limit to how many times one may see a sick person, provided that it does not tire the sick one.
