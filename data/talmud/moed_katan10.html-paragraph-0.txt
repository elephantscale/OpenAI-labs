Moed Katan 10 - More work prohibitions Many types of work are prohibited on the holiday weekdays (chol hamoed) unless they are for the holiday purpose. Even when work is allowed, it should be done unprofessionally. On the other hand, not only baking is permitted, but building an oven or a stove is also permitted. It is a few steps removed from the food preparation for the holidays, but it represents is an exception. It is essential to enjoy the holiday.
