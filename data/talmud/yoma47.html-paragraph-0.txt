Yoma 47 - Kimchit, the flour girl The incense service continues: they bring the High Priest a shovel and a ladle. But he already used the shovel!? - That one was for coals, and this one is with incense, from which he scoops two handfuls, and whether his hands are large or small - that is the measure. Why the ladle? - He puts the incense there - and he cannot do it in any other way because he will need to carry both the incense and the shovel with the coals together into the Holy of Holies.
