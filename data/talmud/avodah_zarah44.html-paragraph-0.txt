Avodah Zarah 44 - How to Properly Dispose of an Idol Rabbi Yose says, "He may grind the idol and scatter it into the wind, or cast it into the sea," but the Sages says that even so it becomes a fertilizer. Their argument is this: both fertilizer and the soil cause the plant to grow. Rabbi Yose says that the combined effect of prohibited and permitted substance is permitted, but the Sages say that it is still prohibited.
