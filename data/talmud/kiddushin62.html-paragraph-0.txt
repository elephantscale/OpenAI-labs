Kiddushin 62 - Mistaken But Valid Betrothals And Those That Can't Happen Yet If one betrothed a woman and subsequently said, "I thought that she was the daughter of a Kohen, but in fact she is the daughter of a Levi", or vice versa; "I thought that she was poor, but in fact she is rich," or vice versa - the woman is betrothed, because she did not mislead him.
