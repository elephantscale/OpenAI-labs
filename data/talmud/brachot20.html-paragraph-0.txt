Brachot 20 - Human Dignity vs Honor of God Rav said: "If one was standing in the street and discovered that his garments had in them "shatnez," a forbidden mixture of wool and linen, he needs to takes them off right there, and disregard his honor." What is Rav's source? - Proverbs, which say, "There is no value to wisdom or understanding against the honor of God." That is, in order not to transgress, one can disregard the honor due his teacher or himself.
