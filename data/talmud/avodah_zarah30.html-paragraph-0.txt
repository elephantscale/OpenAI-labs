Avodah Zarah 30 - Cooked Wine and Wine Left Uncovered If wine is cooked by a Jew, then it never becomes prohibited as "wine of libation." The possible explanations of this are that cooked wine was not used in religious rites, or that cooked wine is an unusual case, and Sages did not promulgate decrees in unusual cases, or that it simply is not wine, but "cooked wine."
