Avodah Zarah 33 - Idol-worship Pilgrimages If a non-Jewish idol-worshipper goes on a pilgrimage, it is forbidden to trade with him when he is on the way there, but permitted when he is on his way back. On the other hand, if a Jewish idol-worshipper is going on a pilgrimage, it is permitted to trade with him on the way there, but forbidden when he is coming back. Why is this difference?
