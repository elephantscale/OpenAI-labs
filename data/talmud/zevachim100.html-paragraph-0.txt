Zevachim 100 - Does the Mourner Eat the Passover Sacrifice? Rabbi Shimon made two statements in two different contexts, that the mourner can eat a Passover offering, and that he cannot. The Talmud gives five possible scenarios of how both can be true, one of them being offered by Abaye: one refers to a case when the mourner's relative died before midday, and the other - when he died after midday.
