Taanit 11 - The fasts of special individuals If the rain did not come as expected in the fall, on the seventeenth of Marcheshvan, special individuals begin to fast on Monday, Thursday and following Monday. They fast only since morning till evening, and they are allowed to bathe and to work. If at the beginning of Kislev, about two weeks later, there are still no rains, the Court decrees three fasts, now already for the community.
