Eruvin 60 - The quandary of Abaye The residents of a town of Kakunaei came to Rav Yosef, asking him to appoint someone to arrange an eruv for them, so that they should be permitted to carry in it on Shabbat. Rav Yosef appointed Abaye, telling him, "Make sure they don't complain." He meant to do it right by properly observing the rules of exclusion.
