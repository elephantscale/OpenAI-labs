Eruvin 16 - Shabbat in a caravan in the desert If a caravan encamps in an open field, one cannot carry inside the caravan's area on Shabbat. Since this area lacks partitions, the Sages gave it the status of "karmelit", which is neither private nor public, but still one cannot carry there. Therefore, they may decide to make a fence around the caravan. Then the following rules apply.
