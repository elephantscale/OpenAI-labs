Shabbat 32 - Protective merits of a person Being careful about three commandments protects a woman in labor: the laws of niddah (here to here), challah (the first portion of the dough, given to a kohen), and kindling of the Shabbat candles. Why are these special? Niddah is naturally connected to childbirth; challah is connected to anything that is first, and Adam was the first; and human soul is called a candle.
