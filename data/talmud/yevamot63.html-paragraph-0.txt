Yevamot 63 - Man must have children A few teachings of Rabbi Elazar about a man and his wife: one who does not have a wife is not a whole man because "God created them male and female, and called their name Man;" a wife is called "helper against him" - if he deserves, she is a helper, if not - she is against him; "God brought her to Adam, and Adam said, "this time it is bone of my bones and flesh of my flesh" teaches that Adam has approached every animal, but his mind was pacified only with Eve.
