Bava Kamma 45 - Custodian Is Responsible for Damages as the Owner If the owner of an animal entrusted it to the care of an unpaid custodian, a borrower, a paid custodian or a renter and then the animal damaged, the custodian is responsible for the damage as if he would have been the owner. Laws of Custodians Unpaid custodian is responsible only for damage resulting from his negligence. Paid custodian is responsible if the item is stolen or lost but not for unavoidable mishaps: an animal that broke a limb, or was captured by armed brigands, or died of natural causes.
