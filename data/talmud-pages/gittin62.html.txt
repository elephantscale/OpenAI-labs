Gittin 62 - Problems of the unlearned A Sage should not help an unlearned person to separate the priest's portion from dough. The unlearned person's dough is presumed to be ritually impure, but the priest might think it pure since it comes from the sage. In contrast, a Sage is allowed to kneed dough for the unlearned, then separate the priest's portion - but teach the unlearned not to touch the dough.
