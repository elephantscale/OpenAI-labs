Kiddushin 74 - Who Is To Be Believed The seller is believed when he states "I sold the item to this person, and the other person gave me the money against my will" - all the time that the merchandise is still in his possession. Midwife is believed to tell us who is the firstborn - but only immediately after birth, his mother is believed for the first seven days of his life, and his father is believed always, because of "...shall recognize the firstborn."
