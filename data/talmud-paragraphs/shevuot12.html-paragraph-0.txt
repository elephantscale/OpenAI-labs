Shevuot 12 - Not Removing Holiness from Sacrifices Earlier Rabbi Yochanan had stated that daily offerings that are not needed by the community can be redeemed. However, Rabbi Shimon disagrees with this. In looking for the opponents of Rabbi Shimon, whose view Rabbi Yochanan follows, we fail to find an explicit recorded ruling and are forced to conclude that Rabbi Yochanan had a tradition from his teachers to this effect.
