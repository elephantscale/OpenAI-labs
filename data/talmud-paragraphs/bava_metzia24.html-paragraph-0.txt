Bava Metzia 24 - Going Beyond the Letter of the Law Rav Yehudah was following behind the master, Shmuel, in the market of cereal stores. He asked Shmuel, "If one found a purse here, what is the law?" Shmuel said, "It belongs to the finder." Many people pass by here, and one of them is likely to take it. Thus, the owner despairs.
