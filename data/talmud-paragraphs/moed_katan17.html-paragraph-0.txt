Moed Katan 17 - The power of the Sages A particular Torah scholar was reported to have inappropriate relationships with young women. Rav Yehudah discussed with his colleagues, "What are we to do? Excommunicate him? - But he is a teacher of many students. Not to excommunicate? Covering up on him is a desecration of God's name!" Rabbah told him in the name of Rabbi Yochanan, "Teacher should be like an angel, and if not - do not study from him." Based on that, they excommunicated him.
