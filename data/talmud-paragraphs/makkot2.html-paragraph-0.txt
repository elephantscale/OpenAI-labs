Makkot 2 - Marital Disqualifications Two people want to disqualify a Kohen by testifying about the marital status of his parents. They come to court and state the following: "We testify about a certain man (who until now was presumed to be a qualified Kohen), that he is a son of a divorced woman. Later they are found to be plotting witnesses: two other witnesses came and ask them, "How can you say that you saw this woman being divorced, when on that day you were with us in a different place?"
