Temurah 10 - Sacrificial Exchange of Limbs One cannot make an exchange of unconsecrated limbs for consecrated fetuses, that is, he cannot say, "Let a leg of this regular animal be substituted for the consecrated fetus that is in the womb of this animal (later one we will discuss how the unborn fetus can become consecrated). Neither can one consecrate unconsecrated fetuses for limbs.
