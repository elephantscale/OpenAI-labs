Eruvin 64 - Nice law Earlier we said that one who does not accept the law of the eruv ruins it for the other residents of the courtyard, making it prohibited for them to carry things in their own courtyard on Shabbat. However, there is a loophole: one can rent this person's rights in the courtyard, and even from his servant. One can also become friendly with the person, do him some service, and be considered his servant for this.
