Kiddushin 41 - Marriage With the Help of an Agent A man can betroth a woman either personally or through his agent. A woman can accept kiddushin and become betrothed either personally or through her agent. A man may give his daughter in betrothal when she is a na'arah (under 12) either personally or through his agent. Performing betrothal as well as any other mitzvah personally is the preferred way.
