Shabbat 56 - Wrong conclusions Rav Shmuel bar Nachmani said in the name of Rabbi Yonatan, "Whoever thinks that Reuven sinned with Bilhah, his father's concubine, is simply mistaken. Rather, he defended his own mother, by moving Jacob's bed out of Bilhah's tent to that of his mother." What is the proof? - In the next Torah phrase, "And the sons of Jacob were twelve" - all equal in statue. However, there is also an opinion that he was greatly tempted but stopped himself, and even another opinion, that he did cohabit with her.
