Eruvin 3 - Partially high enough We learned that an alley requires a beam at its entrance, in order to separate it from the street and to permit carrying in the alley on Shabbat. The beam should be placed no higher than twenty amot (about 40 feet). The same measurement is true for the sukkah - its covering should not be higher than twenty amot. In both cases the reason is that people should notice and be mindful of it.
