Nedarim 45 - When partners fight If two people jointly owned a courtyard - a widespread situation - and then they had a falling out so that each prohibited himself, by a vow, to benefit from his fellow's property, they have a problem entering their own courtyard. Since the courtyard is not divided, each portion of it is jointly owned by both. Thus, when one treads on the ground, he is partially transgressing the property of his fellow, which is prohibited to him by a vow.
