Eruvin 47 - Who does the law follow? The majority of the laws are formulated by the four students of Rabbi Akiva: Rabbi Yose, Rabbi Yehudah, Rabbi Meir, and Rabbi Shimon. Whenever they disagree, the law is decided first in accordance with Rabbi Yose, if he is one of the disputants, then Rabbi Yehudah, and so on, in the order listed above.
