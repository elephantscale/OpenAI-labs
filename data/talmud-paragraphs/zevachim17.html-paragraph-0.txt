Zevachim 17 - Others Who Cannot Perform Sacrificial Service One who is ritually impure today (tevul yom), for example, because of a contact with a dead rat, has to go to a mikveh. Still, some vestiges of ritual impurity linger until nightfall. If he receives the blood of a sacrifice, he makes it invalid, because of a verse "They shall be holy... and they shall not profane." This verse is extra, and we apply it to the one who is partially impure.
