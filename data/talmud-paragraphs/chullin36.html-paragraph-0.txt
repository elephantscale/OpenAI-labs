Chullin 36 - Does the Blood of Shechitah Make Food Susceptible to Impurity? To become susceptible to ritual impurity, food must come into contact with a liquid. The verse "and drinks the blood of its kill" indicates that blood is considered a "beverage," which can prepare food to become impure, but which kind of blood? Let's analyze the following rule. If one slaughtered an animal and the blood spattered on a nearby gourd, then Rabbi Yehudah the Prince says that the gourd can become impure, but his nephew Rabbi Chiya says that its state is in doubt.
