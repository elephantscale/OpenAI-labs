Avodah Zarah 34 - Items of Idol Worshippers that are Permitted Rabbi Akiba visited Ginzak, where they asked him, "Is fasting for part of the day considered religious fasting?" He did not know. They asked, "Earthenware jugs of idol worshippers, are they prohibited or permitted?" He did not know. "In what garments did Moses serve during Tabernacle inauguration?" He did not know.
