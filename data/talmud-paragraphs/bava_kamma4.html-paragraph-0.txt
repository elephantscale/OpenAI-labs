Bava Kamma 4 - How to Translate Mav'eh? Out of the four damagers: ox, pit, man, and fire, the term for "man", or "ma'veh" can have various translations. Rav translates mav'eh as a seeker, or man; Shmuel translates it as "tooth" of an animal consuming produce. Then how does this "tooth" differ from ox? The intent of eating (tooth) is to have pleasure, while the intent of goring (ox) is to harm.
