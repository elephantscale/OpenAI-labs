Shabbat 14 - The Eighteen wins of Beit Shammai The eighteen decrees of Beit Shammai, which they were able to promulgate forcefully, outnumbering Beit Hillel, concern mostly with matters of ritual purity, which are only important in the times of the Temple and in dealing with ritually pure foods.
