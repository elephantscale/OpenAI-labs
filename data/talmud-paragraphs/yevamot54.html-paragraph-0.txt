Yevamot 54 - Erection is a state of mind One who decides to do a yibum - to cohabit with his deceased brother's wife ("yevamah") - can do it in any of the following ways. Whether done willingly for a mitzvah or by coercion or by mistake, whether he only begins the act or completes it, and whether it is natural or anal intercourse - it makes no difference, and he acquires her as a wife.
