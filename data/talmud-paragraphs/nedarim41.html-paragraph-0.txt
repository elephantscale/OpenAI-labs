Nedarim 41 - Moral lessons from Rav Ami Rabbi Ami was quoted earlier, so we continue with his teachings. When the Torah says, "Because you did not serve God when you had everything and lacked nothing, now you will serve your enemies in hunger and cold and nakedness... without anything," what does "without anything" mean? What does it add? - Without a wife, he has been without joy, blessing, goodness, Torah, protection, assistance, atonement, peace, and life, and he is considered only half-man.
