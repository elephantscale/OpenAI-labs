Chullin 56 - These are the Cases of Terefah in Fowl These are the cases of terefah in fowl: a bird whose esophagus was punctured, or one whose trachea was severed. If a weasel struck (bit) it on its head in a place that makes it a terefah, that is, opposite the brain, and there is a concern that the brain membrane may have been punctured. If the gizzard was punctured, or if the intestines were punctured.
