Pesachim 42 - Unwanted substances The following must be removed (from the world) on Passover. Babylonian sour dip, because it had sour milk and moldy breadcrumbs - ingredients about which people tend to forget; beer from Media - because it had some barley added to it, while most beer brands at the time were made from dates; and wine vinegar from Edom - because it, too, had barley.
