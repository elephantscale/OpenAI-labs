Yoma 29 - Things to be done in the morning Earlier, we mentioned how the priests mistook the moon's light for the light of the sun and slaughtered the morning sacrifice before its time. But how was it possible? The light of the moon shows in a column, and the light of the sun is spread out!? - It was a cloudy day. This teaches us an additional lesson: the sun behind the clouds diffuses its heat. Why is that important? - for example, this may affect the leavening of matzah.
