Chullin 57 - Non-Fatal Injuries in Birds Birds have less vitality than animals, as evidenced by the fact that only one of the two pipes - either the trachea or the esophagus - need to be cut for the bird to be considered slaughtered. Because of this we might have thought that injuries similar to those of animals would be fatal in birds. Therefore, we need the rules for birds that are kosher despite being injured, and here they are.
